asDesigned is a proof of concept to show the current limitions of responsive design and to show how it can be solved. And give back the power to the designers so that they can make their design be asDesigned.

Did you ever wanted your web designs to look exactly as you designed them? No matter the device or technical specifications of each? AsDesigned has a solution!

AsDesigned can make your websites look exactly as you designed on any device. SmartPhone, Tablet, Laptop, Desktop, TV or even bigger custom devices.

The simple iPhone and iPad media queries era is over! With much more devices to support we got into a media queries hell! But there is a solution.
